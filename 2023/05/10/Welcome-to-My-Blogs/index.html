Welcome

Hey there, welcome to my blogs! You can call me Leon, or LK if you may.

I am currently a Computer Science & Technology student at Tsinghua University and I will be receiving my bachelor degree next summer(2024).

My personal interest involves game development and rendering. I have been participating in researches involving computer graphics in the lab in our university since 2022.

Additionally I love video games(although I'm pretty lame 🤣). My favorite game is Destiny2 and I'm a Titan-main.

Currently I'm looking for a job opportunity in the game industry. I have experience programming in C/C++, Python, C#/F# and JavaScript. I also know a bit about OpenGL and game engines such as Unity and Godot4. If you think I would fit well in your team, please feel free to contact me!

I can speak English and Chinese and read a little bit of Japanese and Spanish. I may have to use a translator if you wish to reach out to me using languages other than the ones previously mentioned.

What are my blogs about?

In my blogs here I mostly write about rendering. The posts mostly work as my personal notes so there will inevitably be mistakes. I'll fix the mistakes once I have time to do so(if you would be kind enough to point them out). There may also be grammar mistakes or typos, since I'm not a native English speaker.

The stuff I write about is mostly things I've encountered in my research or while exploring the Internet. So I may upload posts in a rather unordered manner.

I am not sure if I will not write about random stuff I met in my real life though. It takes pains to set up another site, after all.

Currently there are not many posts here since I've just managed to set up this site. I'll be working on it.
