Welcome to My Blogs!

Posted on 2023-05-10 Edited on 2024-01-24

Welcome

Hello, welcome to my blogs! You can call me Leon.

I am an undergraduate student at Tsinghua University. I major in Computer Science & Technology and my research interests focus in computer graphics and high-performance computing. I have been participating in research projects in the lab of our university since 2022. I have also worked shortly as an intern at Meituan and Taichi Graphics in 2023.

I love video games and I do have spent a lot of time playing video games. My favorite game is Destiny 2 (I am a titan-main), but I am really not good at PVP (due to poor aiming lol).

I am expecting to graduate from THU this summer (2024) and I am looking for an opportunity to continue my study in pursuit of a Master’s degree. Moreover, I am open to job opportunities involving rendering or game development. I have experiences coding in languages including C/C++, CUDA, Python, JavaScript and C#/F#. I am also familiar with OpenGL and GLSL. If you would like a collaboration with me, feel free to reach out to me.

My native language is Chinese, and (I think) I am also fluent in English. I can also speak a little Japanese and Spanish. I might have to use a translator if you wish to communicate with me in a language with which I’m not familiar.

What are my blogs about (Updated)?

In my blogs I tend to write about things I came across during my work at the lab or places I work part-time, which are mostly related to rendering and HPC. As mentioned before, English is not my native language, so there might be typos or strange expressions in my blogs.

If you have read some of the old blogs, you may have noticed that they are now nowhere to be found. I genuinely apologize for it. For some reason I do not have time nor the opportunity to continue with the series I started a long time ago, so I considered it a better idea to start all over again.
